Who are we?

Ehtami is a smart application designed specifically to support teachers and parents in managing and monitoring students’ daily activities. Through an easy-to-use interface, teachers can add attendance and leave, record activities, and organize classes, while parents can track their children's progress and receive instant notifications. My interest combines ease of organization and effectiveness of communication, to be the ideal link between school and home.
